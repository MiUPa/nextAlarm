Next Alarm is an alarm clock app for people who can't wake up with a normal alarm.

To stop the alarm, you can choose a wake-up challenge:
- Math problems
- Voice recognition
- Shake your phone
- Walk a number of steps

Other features:
- Unlimited alarms
- Repeat by day of week
- Japanese / English

Permissions:
- Notifications: to show alarm notifications
- Exact alarms / wake lock: to ring on time
- Microphone (optional): for the voice challenge
- Activity recognition (optional): for the step challenge
